Truffle suite is undeniably the most-used development framework in blockchain. We use it to deploy and migrate our smart contracts.

While we rely on truffle's framework we didn't want to make a trade-off between ease of use and security.

By combining Truffle's Framework with the Arkane Wallet provider we grant permission based access to our teammates to pre-sign transactions before it is sent to the blockchain.
